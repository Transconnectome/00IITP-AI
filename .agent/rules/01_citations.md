# 01. Citation & Evidence Rules

## 1. "NeuroX" Evidence Standard

- **Titans/SSM Claims**: Any claim about "Titans" or "State Space Models" surpassing Transformers must cite specific papers (e.g., *Dao et al., 2023*, *Behrouz et al., 2024*).
- **Format**:
  - Invalid: "Recent studies show..."
  - Valid: "Recent studies (Smith et al., 2024; DOI:...) show..."

## 2. Biological Plausibility

- **Two-Part Model**: Claims about biology (e.g., "Hippocampal Indexing Theory") must reference neuroscience literature (e.g., *Teyler & DiScenna, 1986*).
- **Multisensory Integration**: Claims about "reliability-weighted integration" must cite *Ernst & Banks (2002)* or similar seminal works.

## 3. Management

- **Matrix**: Update `docs/02_literature/evidence_matrix_ssm.md` before using a new key reference in the proposal draft.
- **Link Rot**: Prefer DOIs (`https://doi.org/...`) over changeable URLs.
